Day 1 of learning Javacript!

Today, learnt datatypes in JS. There are two types of datatypes in JS:

  • Primitive datatype: Primitive datatypes are those datatypes whose variable stores only one value. Example:- Number, boolean, string(" "/' '). There are special primitive datatypes also and they are null and undefined.
  • Non-primitive datatype: the variables of these type of datatypes store more than one values that too of different datatypes. Example:- Array and object.
